Model
Chris

I moved to Amsterdam in March 2025. In September of that year, I began working as a model for art classes. What started as a paid hobby has turned into my full-time job.

Working at the still centre of the creative act

As an art model I collaborate with artists at every level — from beginners at open life-drawing sessions to advanced students at professional academies. Each session is an exercise in sustained stillness, presence, and the quiet dialogue between model and artist.
